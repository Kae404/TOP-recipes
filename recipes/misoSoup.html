Miso Soup

This miso soup is full of savory flavors. Add more dashi to your soup if you want a stronger stock. You can use yellow, white, or red miso paste for this soup â€” yellow miso is sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Miso Paste
  • Silken Tofu
  • Green Onions

Steps:

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in Tofu and green onions
  4. Let simmer for 2 to 3 minuets before serving
