Carne Asada Tacos

These carne asada tacos made with tender strips of flank steak, served on soft corn tortillas are as good as you get at a taqueria. So delicious topped with onion relish and homemade tomato salsa!

Ingredients:

  • Flank Steak
  • Marinade
  • Salsa
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cojita cheese

Steps:

  1. Add Marinade to steak and let sit for 1 to 8 hours
  2. Cut the marinated steak into strips and cook in vegetable oil over medium high heat
  3. Warm tortillas in a skillet over low heat for about a minuet
  4. Place steak, salsa, and cheese on the tortillas. Server with lime wedges
