Spaghetti

It is a family favorite and will not be replaced!Serve over any variety of hot cooked pasta.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • spaghetti
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil and add pasta. Cook for 6-8 minuets and drain
  2. Add ground beef, onions, and garlic to medium high pan. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  3. Add tomato sauce to ground beef and simmer for 20 minuets
  4. Serve with shredded parmesan cheese
